Help Center

FAQ Library

Find answers to commonly asked questions about the Algorithm Client program.

How do I get started with the Algorithm Client program?

To get started, complete the onboarding process by watching all the training videos in order. Once completed, you'll receive access to all member resources and can schedule your setup call with our team. Make sure to review the fundamentals video first as it covers essential concepts you'll need.

What is the recommended starting capital?

The recommended starting capital varies based on your goals and risk tolerance. We recommend discussing this during your setup call where we can provide personalized guidance based on your specific situation and investment objectives.

How do I update my billing information?

To update your billing information, please contact our support team directly. They will securely update your payment details and ensure your account remains in good standing. All billing changes are processed within 24-48 hours.

How does the algorithm work?

Our proprietary algorithm analyzes multiple market indicators and patterns to identify high-probability trading opportunities. The detailed mechanics are covered in our training videos, starting with the Algorithm Fundamentals video in the Video Library.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team through multiple channels. Email us directly, use the live chat feature during business hours, or schedule a call through our booking system. We typically respond within 24 hours on business days.

What should I do after completing onboarding?

After completing onboarding, schedule your setup call to get personalized guidance. Then explore the Video Library to deepen your understanding. We recommend watching at least one video per week and applying the concepts progressively.

What markets does the algorithm cover?

Our algorithm is designed to work across multiple markets including stocks, ETFs, and forex. The specific strategies and market focus will be covered during your setup call and in our advanced training materials.

Can I pause my membership?

Yes, you can pause your membership for up to 3 months. Contact our support team to arrange a pause. During the pause period, you'll retain access to previously unlocked content but won't receive new weekly updates.

What are the support hours?

Our support team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST. For urgent matters outside these hours, you can email us and we'll respond first thing the next business day.

Where can I find the weekly video updates?

Weekly video updates are featured on the main Dashboard page as "Video of the Week." You'll also receive an email notification when new content is published. All past weekly videos are archived in the Video Library.
